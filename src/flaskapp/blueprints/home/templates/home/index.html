Hello User!

In a world where technology is becoming more and more a part of our lives, the danger posed by cyberthreats to which we could be exposed is growing. Knowing how to prevent and act against them is crucial to avoid becoming the victim of cybercriminals. Good practices and general knowledge on how to act in cyberspace should be more widely disseminated to the population, especially to those who do not have sufficient technical knowledge of Information and Communications Technology (ICT). This work aims to provide a tool to raise awareness by bringing them concepts, good practices and practical scenarios to reflect on.
